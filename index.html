What I offer

Get copy that is ready for publishing anywhere

Have you had issues with poor quality translations, or proofreading that just isn’t up to standard? Whether it’s an entire book or just a couple of sentences for your website, if you want high-quality, accurate text that is publication-ready, you have come to the right place. With eleven book credits and counting and a degree in languages and linguistics from the University of Cambridge, I work with companies and private clients all over the world on anything from website copy and literary manuscripts to academic theses and technical guides.
