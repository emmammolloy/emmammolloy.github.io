About


Over five years professional experience in translating, proofreading and editing


I was born in the UK and graduated from the University of Cambridge with a degree in Russian and German. Apart from Cambridge, I have also spent a lot of time in Munich and various cities across Russia, and am currently based in Berlin. My background lies primarily in media, publishing and marketing — and I have translated and edited a number of books. I have worked for companies such as ProSiebenSat.1, teNeues Verlag, NOMOS Glashütte, and the Moscow Polytechnic Museum, translating and editing anything from website copy and literary manuscripts, to more scientific and technical texts.
