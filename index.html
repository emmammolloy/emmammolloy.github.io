Services

Translation



I translate a wide range of texts from German or Russian into British or American English. But a text often cannot be simply translated one-to-one: Cultural differences also need to be taken into account – and this is localisation. So it goes without saying that all my translations are localised accordingly for the target market.

Editing



Have you got a text with details that need double-checking? Or does it just need a bit of polishing up? Editing involves checking the consistency, accuracy and style of a text and the information in it (such as the correctness of names and facts). A bit like proofreading+.

Proofreading



Whether you are handing in your thesis and want a second pair of eyes to take a look, or you have already got a text translated but want another native speaker to check it – my proofreading service involves checking a text for grammar, spelling and punctuation.

About


Over five years professional experience
in translating, proofreading and editing


I came out of the womb obsessed with words and languages. OK, maybe I didn’t know it that early on, but today it is part of what gets me up every morning. I was born in the UK and graduated from the University of Cambridge with a degree in Russian and German. Apart from Cambridge, I have also spent a lot of time in Munich and various cities across Russia and am currently based in Berlin.

I have a background primarily in media, publishing and marketing – and have translated and edited a number of books (see my projects section). I have worked for companies such as ProSiebenSat.1, teNeues Verlag, NOMOS Glashütte, and the Moscow Polytechnic Museum, translating and editing anything from website copy and literary manuscripts, to scientific and legal texts.
