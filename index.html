Over five years professional experience in translating, editing and proofreading


From the UK, I graduated from the University of Cambridge with a degree in Russian and German. With eleven book credits and counting, I edit and translate anything from website copy and literary manuscripts to academic theses and technical guides for companies and private clients worldwide. I have worked for publishers, museums, film festivals, luxury brands, media corporations, and more. I have moved back and forth, living in Munich, Berlin, Ekaterinburg – but am currently settled in Cambridge, UK.
